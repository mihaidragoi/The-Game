🎮 Game Mechanics
The engine enforces the core rules of the game:

The Piles: Two ascending piles (starting at 1) and two descending piles (starting at 100).

The Rule of 10: A unique mechanic allowing players to "jump back" exactly 10 numbers (e.g., placing a 70 on an 80 in an ascending pile) to gain more space.

Turn Logic: Players must discard a minimum of 2 cards per turn until the deck is empty.

Victory Condition: Successfully placing all cards. If a player cannot make a legal move, the game ends.
